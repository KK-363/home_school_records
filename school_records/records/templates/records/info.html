Information

Below there is information on how to use School Records which has been designed to help homeschooling parents keep track of the hours their child(ren) spend on different subjects and activities during the year.

To view all subjects a student is currently taking goto here and add the student's name to the end of the url
Example: /students/Mari/

To view all students currently a specific subject go here and add the subject name to the end of the url
Example: /students/History/

To view all subjects a student is currently taking go here and add the students name to the end of the url
Example: /students/Mari/

To view how much time a student has time on a speficic subject in a certain month/year go here and add the students name, subjects name, year and month to the end of the url
Example: /records/Mari/History/2022/05/
